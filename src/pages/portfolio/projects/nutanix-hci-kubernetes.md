**Client:** Commercial Bank PLC  
**Project Duration:** 15/11/2025 – 01/12/2025

## Overview

Orchestrated the end-to-end deployment of a highly available, 14-node Nutanix Hyperconverged Infrastructure (HCI) solution designed to modernize the bank's core application landscape. The primary objective was to establish a resilient, high-performance foundation to host mission-critical Kubernetes clusters for the bank's cloud-native applications. To meet stringent financial regulatory and operational requirements, the architecture was meticulously segmented to ensure complete isolation between live operations, testing, and failover capabilities.

## Key Features & Contributions

### 🏢 Enterprise HCI Implementation
Installed, initialized, and configured **14 Lenovo ThinkAgile HX650 V3** Nutanix-certified nodes, creating a unified, high-performance compute and storage fabric.

### 🛡️ Strategic Environment Segregation
Architected the physical node distribution into three distinct environments: a 5-node Production cluster for live microservices, a 5-node Disaster Recovery (DR) cluster for site-level redundancy, and a 4-node Non-Production/Development cluster for safe software testing and deployment.

### ☸️ Kubernetes Foundation Optimization
Tuned the underlying Nutanix infrastructure to optimally support scalable, containerized workloads, empowering the bank's software teams to efficiently orchestrate and scale their Kubernetes environments.

### 🔄 High Availability & Resiliency
Established robust replication and failover protocols between the Production and DR clusters, ensuring the bank's critical digital services adhere to strict Recovery Time Objectives (RTO) and Recovery Point Objectives (RPO).

## Impact

Provided Commercial Bank PLC with a modernized, agile, and highly fault-tolerant infrastructure. This deployment successfully bridged physical hardware management with modern DevOps practices, accelerating the bank's transition to microservices while guaranteeing the continuous availability of their next-generation digital banking platforms.
