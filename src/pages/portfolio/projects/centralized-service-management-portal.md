**Association:** Trident Corporation Pvt Ltd.  
**Project Duration:** Jan 2025 – Mar 2026

## Overview

Spearheaded the design and development of a centralized web application to streamline IT service management operations. Recognizing the inefficiencies of managing customer data, support tickets, and hardware inventory across fragmented, shared Excel sheets, this project delivered a unified platform to eliminate data silos and automate administrative workflows within the Datacenter and Infrastructure segment.

## Key Features & Contributions

### 🖥️ Unified Dashboard
Developed a real-time tracking interface displaying overall ticket statuses (Pending, Completed, Hold) and geographical ticket locations across Sri Lanka.

### 🗄️ Customer & Asset Management
Engineered comprehensive database structures to link customer profiles directly to their registered datacenter devices (e.g., Lenovo ThinkSystem, HPE Synergy) and specific hardware components.

### 🎫 Ticketing & Helpdesk System
Created a robust support portal that logs historical data, categorizes issues by priority, and tracks resolution times, drastically reducing the time previously wasted searching through older documents.

### 📦 Inventory Automation
Implemented bulk CSV upload functionality to efficiently manage and update server part lists and stock.

### 🚀 Deployment
Successfully hosted the application on a **Lenovo ThinkSystem SR530** running a **Windows IIS Server**.

## Impact

Transformed a manual, spreadsheet-heavy process into a streamlined, single-console digital workflow. This significantly reduced administrative overhead, accelerated the retrieval of historical service data, and improved overall response times for datacenter customer support.
